with database..
-Hibernate is ORM(object relational mapping)tool
-Hibernate is a open source,lightweight,free of cost..
-Hibernate is a non  invasive framework,means it wont forces in the programm to 
 exends or implements any classes or interface in the hibernate programm..
-It is invented by Gavin King in 2001.
-Any type of application can build with hibernate Framework..
